HOW TO PLAY

  • Turn-based flick football — like Soccer Stars. You and the opponent take turns flicking discs.
  • Flick: press & hold one of your discs, drag back to aim & load power (the arrow & ring show it), then release to fire it. Knock the ball into their net.
  • Possession: you keep the ball only if your disc touched it and your team is closest to it when everything stops. If you didn't touch it, or the other team's disc ends up nearest, it becomes their ball. So control it — don't just smash it away.
  • Formations: instead of flicking, tap DEFENSIVE / NORMAL / ATTACKING up top to reset your whole team's shape — deep, midfield or pushed up. It shifts toward the ball, and it uses your turn. On your own throw-in or corner you can re-shape as much as you like before taking it — it stays your restart.
  • Your keeper you can grab and drag anywhere inside your box any time — cover a shot from any angle. It can't be flicked.
  • Smashing an opponent before the ball is a foul → free kick, or a penalty in the box. Ball off the sides → throw-in; off the ends → corner or goal kick (opponents must stand clear).
  • Career: £42M to build an 11-man squad — every slot gives you 10 players; higher ratings flick harder & truer. Group stage → knockouts → the final. Prize money rolls into next season.
  • Two halves, a running clock, and a match timer. Set AI difficulty — Easy, Medium, Hard, or Impossible — in Settings.
