HOW TO PLAY

  • Run: HOLD MOUSE — your player (gold ring) runs toward the cursor.
  • Attacking: SPACE pass toward the cursor · SPACE ×2 through-ball into space · HOLD SPACE shoot — a power bar fills at the top. More power = less accurate: a full-blooded hit can fly wide, a placed shot stays true.
  • Defending: SPACE slide tackle · SPACE ×2 switch to the best-placed defender (picked automatically from where the ball and players are).
  • Keepers are AI — yours dives better if you bought a better one.
  • Smashing a player without winning the ball = foul → free kick, or a penalty in the box.
  • Career: £42M to build an 11-man squad — every slot gives you 10 players to choose from; ratings make them genuinely faster, stronger, sharper. Group stage → knockout rounds → the final. Prize money rolls into next season.
  • Set difficulty and half length (3–10 min) in Settings.
