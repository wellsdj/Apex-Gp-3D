HOW TO PLAY

  • You play the highlights. Each match is a handful of key moments (4–15). Your team walks out, then you're dropped into the first one.
  • Attacking: the moment freezes — draw with the mouse where the ball should go. Draw to a teammate to pass; draw at the goal for a shot. If your player receives it, you draw again — keep the move going until you score or lose it.
  • Lose the ball to a defender and you get 3 seconds to win it back — draw a run to chase it down. If they clear it, the highlight's over.
  • Defending: when the AI is about to pass, it freezes — draw your defender's run to cut it out (they move at their own pace). When they're about to shoot, drag your keeper to a side to make the save.
  • You don't have to score every highlight — the final score is whatever actually happens across them.
  • Career: £42M to build an 11-man squad — every slot gives you 10 players to choose from; better ratings really do play better. Group stage → knockouts → the final. Prize money rolls into next season.
  • Set difficulty in Settings.
