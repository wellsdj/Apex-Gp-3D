HOW TO PLAY

  • Click & hold one of your players when it's your turn.
  • Pull back — the further you pull, the more power you load.
  • The arrow shows where your player will fly. Release to flick!
  • Smash the ball into the other team's net. First to 3 wins.
  • Teams take turns — one flick each. Use your whole squad: defenders, keeper, anyone.
